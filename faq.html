1. What is the website about?
This website is a student project that shows basic web pages like home, FAQ, inventory, and reviews.
2. How can I contact you?
You can contact us by email at shop.stationarywonderland@gmail.com. For more information, visit the Contact Us page.
3. Do you offer shipping?
Yes, we offer local shipping within Qatar. Delivery usually takes 3–5 working days.
4. What payment methods do you accept?
We currently accept cash on delivery and credit card payments.
5. Can I return a product?
Yes, you can return products within 7 days if they are in their original condition.
6. How long does delivery take?
Most orders arrive within 1–3 days depending on your location.
7. Do you restock sold-out items?
Yes, popular items are restocked regularly. You can message us to check availability.
8. Are the product colors exactly like the pictures?
We try our best to show accurate colors, but lighting and screen settings may cause small differences.
9. Can I order without creating an account?
Yes! You can place an order as a guest without logging in.
10. How do I leave a review?
Go to the product page, scroll to the review section, and click “Write a Review.” You can also upload images.
11. What should I do if I receive a damaged item?
Please contact us within 24 hours with photos, and we will replace the item.
12. Do you offer discounts or promotions?
Yes! We offer seasonal sales, special deals, and exclusive discounts for subscribers.
